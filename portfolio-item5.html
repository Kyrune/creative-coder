In the Midst

COVID-19 Hackathon Project

GitHub Repository

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I joined a hackathon where I became part of a remote team. Over the course of the week-long hackathon, I worked with team members to build a MERN stack website with the goal of providing a way for local communities and churches to supply those at risk with resources, as well as giving members the opportunity to donate.

I worked mainly on the front-end, pair programming with team members as we sought solutions together. I also created the website's name, In the Midst, signifying that even in the midst of chaos, there is hope.

I also designed the logo image, both in the navigation bar and the footer, to reflect the meaning of the website's title, connecting it to the "i" in "Midst" to symbolize that in the "eye" of the storm, there can be peace and hope.
