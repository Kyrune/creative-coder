I worked as a freelance web developer with a remote Agile team, composed of fellow Bethel Tech graduates. You can check out our LinkedIn profiles here!

Maria Manzella (that's me!) - Web Developer

We worked with a client who has been in the medical field for thirteen years, customizing his website to reflect his goal to bridge the world between medical and legal by providing medical legal consulting in matters pertaining to medical malpractice, workers' compensation, personal injury, and medical device liability.

As the team's primary web developer, I not only customized the website with code, but also optimized the images, edited images and logos as needed through Photoshop, pair programmed with team members, kept the code organized with comments and structure, researched questions posed by team members, and proofread our content writer's work at her request.
